How Extra Commission Works:
  • When students use your assigned coupons, you earn extra commission equal to the coupon's discount percentage on top of your regular commission.
  • Example: If a student uses a 10% discount coupon on a ₹1,000 course:
    • Final price after discount: ₹900
    • Your extra commission: 10% of ₹900 = ₹90
    • This is added to your regular commission!
  • The remaining revenue is split according to your default commission rate set by the platform admin.
